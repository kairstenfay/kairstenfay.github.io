About Me

Data scientist, biologist, investor, teacher, and traveler

I am a passionate problem solver and self-learner with a logic-forward mind and a desire to know the truth. As a data scientist, I want to help solve many of the worlds biggest problems, from economic growth and development to public health, environmental issues, and more.

I have arrived at this point in my career largely through studying full-time in a DIY-style bootcamp. I use Coursera modules, Kaggle competitions, and other open-source, online resources. In five years, I see myself working in private ecological research, diving once again into the field I studied as an undergraduate with my newly acquired skills.

In my free time, I enjoy social dancing, cooking, reading, and investing money in the stock market, especially in socially responsible funds. I'm a savvy self-starter and have even dabbled in some entrepreneurial pursuits, such as running my own pet care business on the side or selling handmade earrings through my Etsy shop.

Thanks for visiting my website.
