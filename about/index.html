About Me


Hi, I'm Kairsten (prounced CARE-stin). I work in that delightfully blurry line between software engineering and data science. I am passionate about tool building on both the front end and back end. I currently work as a full stack web developer at the Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation. I previously worked as a data analyst on the dietary risk factors team.

I am a storyteller, a tool builder, and an avid autodidact with a desire to know the truth. In my free time, I am constantly learning new languages and libraries, building visualizations, and improving my code readability and re-usability. I grew up on the East coast and am currently based in Seattle.

I occasionally do freelance Tableau dashboard development. Please email me at kairsten.fay@gmail.com to schedule an informational. Thanks for visiting my website.
